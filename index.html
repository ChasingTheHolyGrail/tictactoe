Game Rules

Tic Tac Toe is a classic strategy game for two players.

Objective:

Be the first to place three of your symbols in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

Gameplay:

  • You play as X against the computer (O)
  • You start and make the first move
  • After your move, the computer automatically makes its move
  • The winner is whoever gets three in a row first
  • If the board is full without a winner: Draw

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • 1-9: Quick access to cells
  • Ctrl+Z / Cmd+Z: Undo move
  • R: New game
